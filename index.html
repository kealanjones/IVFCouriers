WORLDWIDE MEDICAL COURIER SERVICE


We provide specialist service in cryogenically frozen transfer of sperm, eggs, embryos and cells.


What we do


IVF Couriers offer a specialised personal transport service, providing international ‘hand-carry’ transfers of IVF frozen embryos, sperm, eggs and cells between fertility clinics and medical laboratories worldwide. We specialise in shipping IVF samples that are cryopreserved and temperature-sensitive. Our personal service is recommended for any life science in-vitro samples that are irreplaceable, and therefore precious cargo. Find out more here.

Why choose us


Over 9 years of experience

IVF Couriers has been succesfully transporting cryogenically preserved samples for over 9 years, establishing itself as the leading medical courier service for reproductive cells. Our team has over 50 years of valuable combined experience in biomedical logistics, international customs, and trade body negotiation.

During transfer, nothing leaves our side

We understand how important the samples we transport are. That's why we offer a unique hand-carry service, where we personally collect and carry your precious cargo from A to B by hand, with the utmost of care and attention. We use the latest model Dry Shippers which are approved for carriage in the airplane cabin. This way, we can guarantee safety and ensure absolutely no X-ray at any time during transit. This also eliminates risk of equipment being dropped or damaged by flight handlers.

A personalised service

Our goal at IVF Couriers is to offer a premium alternative to traditional shipping companies, and more importantly, a very personal service during a time that is often filled with many emotions and stress. We will adapt and personalise each assignement based on your needs and requirements, whilst also taking into account the many ethical and religious constraints in order to safely deliver your special consignment.

100% of our transfers safely completed

The samples we transport are precious cargo, and often irreplaceable. That's why we adhere to strict quality controle procedures and extensive training for our couriers, as there is no room for error. As a result of our high standards, we are proud to say that 100% of our transfers have been completed succesfully and safely.
